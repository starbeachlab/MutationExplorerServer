  • Is it possible to mutate peptides?
    The smaller the peptide the more likely it is to be flexible. MutationExplorer has no restrictions concerning the size of the protein being uploaded. However, you should be aware that any conformation is just a snapshot and the smaller your peptide the more likely a given conformation may not represent the effect of a mutation on another conformation.
  • Is it possible to mutate transmembrane proteins?
    In general yes. However, there is no energetic effect due to the lipid bilayer considered at this point.
